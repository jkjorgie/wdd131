Site Name

"Professional Golf" was chosen because the site will be about professional golf, its players, its tours, its news, etc.

Site Purpose

Allow users to learn basic information about the PGA and LIV tours, view info about some of the most popular players, and link to relevant news articles.

Scenarios

  • Who are the most popular professional golfers?
  • What do I need to know about the PGA and LIV tours?
  • What is the latest professional golf news?
